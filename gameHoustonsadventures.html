Houston's Adventures

Unity C# Adobe Photoshop

2D Platformer prototype. It has a set of rooms that are randomly selected from the pool. Each room has a different type of environment, set of enemies and obstacles. Your goal is to get through the rooms as fast as possible and get the highest score. Defeat enemies and collect dropped orbs for points.

  • - Player controller: double jump, dash, groundcheck, hit knockback, diagonal aiming
  • - Unity new input system
  • - Camera controller with lookahead, boundaries and parallax
  • - Rigged player sprite with separate upper and lower body animations
  • - Wind and dissolve shader effects
