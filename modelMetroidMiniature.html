Metroid Miniature

Blender Adobe Substance 3D Painter Adobe Substance 3D Designer

3D miniature model of area from the Super Metroid game. This was a practice to learn Blender and Adobe Substance 3D Painter texture workflow. These are created to be like game assets that are low poly and modular.

Walls and pillars share the same trim sheet texture. Mushroom normal maps were baked in Painter from a high poly sculpt. It is created from 3 parts from which multiple variations can be quickly formed.

Low poly model with normal map and high poly model.
