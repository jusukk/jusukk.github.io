About

This is a simple game made by using JavaScript and HTML5 canvas. Scores are stored into a Firebase Realtime Database and are used to create canvas charts by using a Chart.js library.

The first chart shows top 5 scores. The second chart shows avarages and how many scores have the same value. You can toggle the chart by clicking its label.

Gameplay

Press SPACE to hover and try to avoid obstacles. You score when obstacles reach the other side. The game ends when you hit the obstacle and your score is then send to the database.
