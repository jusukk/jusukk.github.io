Houston's Adventures

Unity

C#

Photoshop

Prototype of a 2D platformer that I made in 2 months. It had a set of rooms that were randomly selected from the pool. Each room had a different type of environment, set of enemies, and obstacles. The player's goal was to get through the rooms as fast as possible and get the highest score. Defeated enemies dropped orbs that could be collected for points.

  • - Player controller: double jump, dash, groundcheck, hit knockback, diagonal aiming, health
  • - Unity input system
  • - Camera controller with a lookahead, boundaries, and a parallax
  • - 2D lights and particle systems
  • - Rigged player sprite with separate upper and lower body animations made in Unity
  • - Sprites made in Photoshop
  • - Wind and dissolve shaders
