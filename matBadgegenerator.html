Badge Generator

Adobe Substance 3D Designer Adobe Photoshop

This was my attempt to create a badge generator in Adobe Substance 3D Designer. It combines height maps that are created procedurally in Designer and Adobe Photoshop.

You can use sliders to change different parts of the badge and easily create multiple different variations. You can also easily add new shapes and materials to it and render them using the PBR Render node.
